About Health Calculators
Health Calculators is an all-in-one suite for health and fitness related calculators that brings the most reliable formulas to one platform.
Basal Metabolic Rate
Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the minimal amount of energy expenditure while at rest.

- Based on the Mifflin St. Jeor equation
Caloric Intake
Your daily caloric needs to maintain your weight is equal to your BMR, multiplied by your activity factor.

- Based on the Mifflin St. Jeor equation
Body Mass Index
Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight.

- Based on the Adolphe Quetelet equation
Fat Free Muscle Index
Fat Free Muscle Index (FFMI) is a measure of muscle mass based on height, weight, and body fat.

- Based on the Kouri, E.M. equation
Lean Body Mass
Lean Body Mass (LBM) is a measure of your body weight minus your body fat.

- Based on the Hume equation
One Repetition Maximum
One Rep Max (1RM) is the maximum amount of force that can be generated in one full contraction.

- Based on the Brzycki equation
Target Heart Rate
Target Heart Rate (THR) is a desired heart rate range reached during an exersize.

- Based on the Haskell and Fox equation
